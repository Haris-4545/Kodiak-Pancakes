Kodiak Chocolate Chip Pancakes

This is a guide on how to make Kodiak Chocolate Chip Pancakes. This might be useful if you threw out the box and forgot the ingredients and their correct ratios.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Kodiak Chocolate Chip Flapjack & Waffle Mix
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of milk or water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 to 2 tbsp of vegetable oil (for the pan)

Instructions

  1. Pour the pancake mix into a large bowl
  2. Pour the milk or water into the bowl
  3. Crack the egg and add it to the bowl (optional)
  4. Mix for ~1 minute with a spoon or mixer
  5. Pour the vegetable oil onto the pan evenly
  6. Set the stove to medium
  7. Pour the pancake mix onto the pan evenly
  8. Wait 3 minutes until you can see visible bubbles before using a spatula to flip the pancake to the other side
  9. Wait 2 to 3 minutes until the pancake is cooked and move it to your plate. Make sure to be careful and not burn yourself.
